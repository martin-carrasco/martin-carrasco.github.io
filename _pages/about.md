I am an [ELLIS](https://ellis.eu/phd-postdoc) Doctoral Student at [AIDOS](https://aidos.group), supervised by [Prof. Bastian Rieck](https://bastian.rieck.me) and co-supervised by [Prof. Søren Hauberg](https://www2.compute.dtu.dk/~sohau/). My research focuses on *geometrical* and *topological* methods in machine learning.


I'm particularly interested in representation learning. In this setting, topologyand geometry are characteristics to conserve or discard depending on our task. Additionally, given my training as computer scientist like algorithmics and graph theory and try to find an intersection of some of this topoics in my research endevoars.

I think there are a lot of lessons to be learned from the comp. geometry and comp. topology communities to grow as a field in machine learning so I try to lean more towards that side.
