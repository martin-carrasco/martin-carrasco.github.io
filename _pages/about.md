## Current work & Thesis
For my M.Sc. thesis I was supervised by [Prof. Erik Bekkers](https://ebekkers.github.io) from [AMLab](http://amlab.science.uva.nl) and [Prof. Bastian Rieck](https://bastian.rieck.me). I explored an alternative metric that fully characterizes attributed graphs, it's relationship to homomorphism counts and how it can, in expectation, completly characterize these objects.

### My current interests

My thesis topic revolves around the limitations in the current expressivity meassures used to quantify the expressive power of GNNs. I believe that we are being too "square" when looking at graphs and we should step out of the box since this expressivity has some practical limitaitons.
