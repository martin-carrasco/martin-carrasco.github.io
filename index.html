I am an ELLIS Doctoral Student at AIDOS, supervised by Prof. Bastian Rieck and co-supervised by Prof. Søren Hauberg. My research focuses on geometrical and topological methods in machine learning.

I’m particularly interested in representation learning. In this setting, topology and geometry are characteristics to conserve or discard depending on our task. Additionally, given my training as computer scientist like algorithmics and graph theory and try to find an intersection of some of these topics in my research endevoars.

I believe there is a lot to learn from the comp. geometry and comp. topology communities to grow as a field (in machine learning). So, I try to lean more towards that type of research.
